description: Analyze what you give up by choosing this path
argument-hint: [choice or leave blank for current context]
---

<objective>
Apply opportunity cost analysis to $ARGUMENTS (or the current discussion if no arguments provided).

Every yes is a no to something else. What's the real cost of this choice when you account for what you're giving up?
</objective>

<process>
1. State the choice being made
2. List all resources it requires (time, money, attention, energy)
3. Identify the best alternative use of those same resources
4. Compare the value of this choice vs the best alternative
5. Determine whether the tradeoff is worth it
</process>

<output_format>
**Choice:** [what you're considering]

**Resources Required:**
- Time: [amount]
- Money: [amount]
- Attention/Energy: [amount]
- Other: [what else it costs]

**Best Alternative Use:**
[What else you could do with those same resources]

**Comparison:**
- This choice delivers: [value]
- Alternative delivers: [value]
- Net tradeoff: [what you gain vs lose]

**Verdict:**
[Is the tradeoff worth it? Why or why not?]
</output_format>

<success_criteria>
- Compares against best alternative, not any alternative
- Makes hidden costs explicit (not just money)
- Accounts for time, attention, and opportunity — not just dollars
- Reveals when "affordable" things are actually expensive
- Enables genuine value comparison, not isolated assessment
</success_criteria>
